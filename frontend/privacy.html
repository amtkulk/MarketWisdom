Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Welcome to Market Wisdom. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information Collection

Market Wisdom is a financial data aggregator and news reader. We do not require users to create an account, and we do not actively collect personal identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. All market research and screener tools are provided anonymously.

2. Data Usage

Our application fetches financial data from public APIs and our own backend servers. We may collect anonymous, non-identifiable usage data (such as crash reports and basic analytics) solely for the purpose of improving the performance and stability of the application.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app displays live feeds from public channels (such as Telegram) and financial news APIs. These third-party services may have their own privacy policies. We do not share any of your personal data with these third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any data transmitted between your device and our servers using standard encryption protocols (HTTPS).

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official website.
